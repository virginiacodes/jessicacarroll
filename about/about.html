About

Jessica is an actor and versatile vocal chameleon from London. She was raised in a theatre and performed in plays and musicals as a teen before going on to train at London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA) where she represented the school in the Carleton Hobbs Radio Drama Competition and found a love for the voice world. With a lot of grafting, Jessica has carved out an extensive voice career for herself in videogames, commercials, cartoons, radio, dubbing and is a regular in the TV and Film ADR circuit.

Blessed with an ear for accents and creating characters (according to her Mum she's been mimicking people and parroting dialects since she was tiny) Jessica spends most days recording in USA, Irish, Australian, London and even old-fashioned Norfolk, amongst a bunch of others.

It turns out she's an 'excellent screamer' which is very handy in the gaming world! Some of her recent video game work includes Disciple Z'rell in multi-award-winning Baldur's Gate 3 and several leads and hundreds of weird and wonderful characters in the BAFTA-winning Divinity: Original Sin 2, Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire, Elex, Spellforce 3, Dragon Quest XI, Unforeseen Incidents and Code 7.

In the Cartoon world, Jessica voices Darcy the Digger, Riff & Riff and Farona in Thomas & Friends (Amazon/Netflix) and had the joy of creating Maureen the Rat in some BBC Teach cartoons. She also sings and usually has a song or two in the cartoons she voices.

Over a decade ago, Jessica was lucky enough to discover the world of TV and Film post-production (ADR) and can be heard on most channels screaming, crying, reading news reports, chatting down police radios, singing and everything in between on TV shows such as One Day, Baby Reindeer, Supacell, The Regime, Happy Valley, Chernobyl, Black Mirror, Top Boy, Peaky Blinders, It's A Sin, I May Destroy You, Downton Abbey, The Crown, Line of Duty, Vikings, Killing Eve, Valhalla, His Dark Materials, The Missing, Taboo, Call The Midwife, The Last Kingdom, Game of Thrones, Dr Foster… and films including Pain Hustlers, Canary Black, Heart of Stone, Bond, Mary Poppins Returns, Bridget Jones, The Swimmers, The Northman…She even got to play the A.I in Daniel Kaluya's The Kitchen alongside Kano!

You've definitely heard Jessica on a commercial or two! Clients include Rimmel, Marriott, Vision Express, Mugler, Nutella, Ninja Warrior, Nature Valley, President Cheese, Lovehoney, Bamboo, Vaseline, Disney, Palmers Coco Butter, O'Keefe's Lip Repair, Glitter Bingo, Protein World, Invisalign, Photobox, The Saucy Fish Company, Fitbit, Google, EE, Microsoft, the UN, Siemens, Vodafone, the National Grid, Panasonic, Novartis and National Geographic and many, many more.

As a highly-skilled dubber, Jessica's been enjoying voicing the UK dubs of various programmes and films on Netflix including Criminal: Germany, Caliphate, Freud, Faking Hitler, Asterix & Obelisk, Hildegart, Tore, Tear Along The Dotted Line, to name a few.

She's been in some lovely radio dramas including Life Begins at Crawley andThe Future of Radio (Radio 4); The British Are Coming and Liberation Is Not A Recognised Protocol (Apple).

Jessica's theatre credits include Fence (Finborough); Fishskin Trousers (The Park Theatre, Finborough); The Broken Token (Theatre Royal Bury St Edmunds, Lakeside, William Andrews Clark - Los Angeles); Quirks (Southwark Playhouse); Old Bag (Theatre 503); Ghosts (Battersea Arts Centre); Hellcab (Old Red Lion); Last Seen (Almeida); The Woman of No Importance (Assembly Rooms Ludlow); Taking Steps (Assembly Rooms Ludlow); Daisy Pulls It Off (Lyric Hammersmith).

She also has a professional home recording studio!
